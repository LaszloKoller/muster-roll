Round 6 of the BaBR Ladder Tournament began on Monday, October 25, 2010. All match bids were finalized by (or before) Sunday, October 31, 2010 at 11pm EDT with the lower ranked player of each match pair (that is, the player with the even-numbered rank) offering the initial bid. Round 6 ended on Friday, February 25, 2011 at 11pm EST (having lasted approximately 4 months).
