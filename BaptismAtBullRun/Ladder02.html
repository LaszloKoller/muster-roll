Round 2 of the BaBR Ladder Tournament began on Monday, January 26, 2009. All match bids were finalized by (or before) Monday, February 2, 2009 at 11pm EST with the lower ranked player of each match pair (that is, the player with the even-numbered rank) offering the initial morale bid. Round 2 ended on Friday, May 1, 2009 at 11pm EDT (having lasted approximately 3 months).
